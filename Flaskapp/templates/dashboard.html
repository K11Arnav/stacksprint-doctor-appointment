Health Tips

Stay Hydrated

Drink at least 2 to 3 liters of water daily to stay energized and support your immune system.

Regular Exercise

Engage in 30 minutes of physical activity at least 5 days a week for a healthy heart and mind.

Eat Fresh

Include fruits, vegetables, and whole grains in your diet. Avoid processed and junk food.

Get Enough Sleep

Aim for 7 to 8 hours of quality sleep to improve mental focus and reduce health risks.
